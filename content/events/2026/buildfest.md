## About Buildfest

**Buildfest** is a month-long celebration of open source software. Whether you're a seasoned contributor or just starting out, this contest provides a platform to contribute to meaningful projects, learn from mentors, and connect with the global open source community.

### Contest Format

- **Duration:** 1 Month (March 20 - April 20, 2026)
- **Eligibility:** Open to all students and developers
- **Contribution:** Pull requests to participating repositories
- **Rewards:** Quality contributions earn points towards leaderboards

### How to Participate

1.  **Register:** Sign up on the official portal.
2.  **Pick Issues:** Browse the list of participating projects and issues.
3.  **Contribute:** Submit PRs and get them merged.
4.  **Win:** Top contributors win exclusive swag and prizes.

### Why Join?

- **Learn:** Gain real world development experience.
- **Network:** Connect with project maintainers and other devs.
- **Swag:** Earn limited edition t-shirts, stickers, and more.
